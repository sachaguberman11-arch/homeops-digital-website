FAQ

How fast will I see results?
Most clients see their first leads within the first week of launch. Google Ads works on search intent. People are already looking. We put you in front of them.
Do I need a website?
No. We build the landing pages as part of setup. If you have an existing site, we can work with it. The landing pages are what ads point to, and we build those from scratch.
What trades do you work with?
Painters, roofers, landscapers, concrete, HVAC, window and door companies, and other home service businesses. If your jobs come from local homeowners, this works for you.
How much should I spend on ads?
Most clients start at $1,000–2,000 CAD/month in ad spend. That's separate from the management fee. We'll figure out a starting number together on the call based on your market and goals.
How do I know it's working?
Every lead is tracked: calls, form fills, everything. You get a weekly report with the numbers. You'll always know exactly what you're getting for your spend.
What if I already tried Google Ads and it didn't work?
It usually comes down to two things: wrong keywords, or ads pointing to a homepage instead of a purpose-built landing page. That's exactly what we fix.
